For Outside Locations

(Beach, Park etc)

  • Zoom in on your blue location dot
  • Let the dot ‘settle’ in position
  • Line up the pink pin with the centre of dot and click Done to lock in your what3words location delivery address

For Inside Locations

(Home, Office etc)

  • The blue dot will show your building location
  • Line up the pink pin to the doorway area you require delivery to and click Done to lock in your what3words location delivery address

    • Accuracy is to within a few metres however your delivery person will call you on arrival to your location
