  • Q: Can l drive part-time?

    A: Yes we provide you with an opportunity to work full-time or part-time.

  • Q: When do l get my payout?

    A: iDeliverd pay you weekly to your nominated bank account, or instantly through iDeliverd Money.

  • Q: Does iDeliverd take commission?

    A: Yes you will pay 10% for every trip you provide on iDeliverd platform as a driver.

    A: You should expect 1-2 business days before approval and able to accept order requests.
